Report Management enables your team to compile spreadsheets, run medical audits, and produce PDF files from PC Connect data. The view provides a central entry point for triggering report generation jobs, keeping all output compilation in one place rather than scattered across individual modules.

## Navigation

  <Card title="Dashboard Home" icon="house" href="#dashboard">

  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## What Report Management Does

From this view you can initiate the compilation of three output types:

- **Spreadsheets** — tabular exports of your operational data.
- **Medical audits** — structured audit documents derived from clinical and billing records.
- **PDF files** — formatted document exports ready for distribution or archiving.

## Compiling a Spreadsheet

Click the **Compile Spreadsheet** button to start a spreadsheet compilation job for the current dataset.

```tsx
<Button variant="contained" color="primary">
  Compile Spreadsheet
</Button>
```

## CSV Exports in Individual Modules

<Note>
  Quick CSV exports are also available directly within individual modules. The **Active Incomplete Cases** view exports filtered case data via its toolbar **Export** button, and the **Users Management** and **Client Management** views include their own per-table export controls. Use those in-module exports for targeted, filtered downloads, and use Report Management for broader compiled outputs.
</Note>

<Tip>
  Use Report Management when you need a consolidated output that spans multiple data sources or requires audit formatting, rather than a raw data dump from a single table.
</Tip>
