The Enrollment Dashboard is your central hub for tracking the state of patient enrollments and clinical files across your organization. It surfaces high-level statistics at a glance, helping administrators and enrollment coordinators quickly identify where activity is concentrated and whether any files require attention — without needing to drill into individual records.

## What the Dashboard Shows

Navigating to **ENROLLMENT → Dashboard** (path `#enrollment-dashboard`) loads a summary view with visual statistics for active patient enrollments and clinical files. The description rendered in the dashboard reads:

> *Visual statistics for active patient enrollments and clinical files.*

This single-pane view is designed to give a real-time pulse on enrollment status without requiring manual report generation.

## Enrollment Overview Card

At the center of the dashboard is the **Enrollment Overview** card. It consolidates enrollment data into one focal area and provides a direct entry point into deeper analysis.

<Note>
  The **View Full Report** button inside the Enrollment Overview card opens the full enrollment report, giving you a detailed breakdown of all patient and clinical file records.
</Note>

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the Enrollment section">
    In the left sidebar, locate the **ENROLLMENT** group and click **Dashboard**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review the statistics">
    The dashboard loads visual statistics summarizing active patient enrollments and clinical files.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Access the full report">
    Inside the **Enrollment Overview** card, click **View Full Report** to navigate to the complete enrollment data view.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Navigation

The Enrollment Dashboard is accessible from the sidebar under the **ENROLLMENT** group:

| Sidebar Group | Item      | Path                   |
| ------------- | --------- | ---------------------- |
| ENROLLMENT    | Dashboard | `#enrollment-dashboard` |
