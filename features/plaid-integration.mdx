The Plaid section provides a secure way to connect a bank account to PC Connect for financial verification and transaction processing. PC Connect integrates with [Plaid](https://plaid.com), a widely adopted third-party financial data platform, to handle the bank account linking flow without exposing sensitive credentials to the application directly.

<Info>
  Plaid is a third-party service. Bank account linking is handled entirely within Plaid's own secure interface. PC Connect does not store or process raw banking credentials.
</Info>

## Accessing the Plaid section

Navigate to **ADMIN → Plaid** in the left-hand sidebar to open this section. The section is available to users with Admin-level permissions.

## Link Account Credentials card

The main content area contains the **Link Account Credentials** card. This card is the single entry point for initiating a bank account connection through Plaid Link.

<CardGroup cols={1}>
  <Card title="Link Account Credentials" icon="building-columns" href="#launch-plaid-link">
    Initiates the Plaid Link flow to connect a verified bank account. Click **Launch Plaid Link** inside this card to begin.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## What is Plaid Link?

Plaid Link is Plaid's embedded, browser-based module that guides users through securely selecting their financial institution and authorizing account access. The flow runs entirely within Plaid's interface — PC Connect only receives a verified account token once the process is complete, which is then used for financial verification and transaction processing settings within the platform.

## Linking a bank account

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate to Admin > Plaid">
    Open the sidebar and go to **ADMIN → Plaid**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click Launch Plaid Link">
    Inside the **Link Account Credentials** card, click the **Launch Plaid Link** button. Plaid's secure bank-linking interface will open.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Complete Plaid's secure bank-linking flow">
    Follow the on-screen prompts inside the Plaid interface: select your financial institution, authenticate with your banking credentials, and authorize the account connection. Your credentials are submitted directly to Plaid and are never passed through PC Connect.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  Once the Plaid Link flow is completed successfully, the linked account credentials will be available to PC Connect for bank account verification and transaction processing configuration.
</Note>
