ED Floor Visits provides a real-time feed of patient floor visits and medical device allocations across emergency department units. The view gives care coordinators and clinical staff an up-to-date snapshot of active patient movement and equipment usage without needing to leave the PC Connect dashboard.

## What This View Shows

The ED Floor Visits view surfaces two key data streams:

- **Patient floor visits feed** — real-time records of patients currently placed on ED floors, updated as visit data changes.
- **Medical device allocations** — equipment assigned to patients or floor bays at the time the feed was last loaded.

<Info>
  This is a live-feed view. The data displayed reflects the state of the system at the moment the feed was last loaded or refreshed. It does not update automatically in the background — use the **Refresh Visit Feed** button to pull the latest records.
</Info>

## Refreshing the Feed

Click the **Refresh Visit Feed** button to reload the latest patient floor visits and device allocation data from the source system.

```tsx
<Button variant="contained" color="primary">
  Refresh Visit Feed
</Button>
```

<Tip>
  Refresh the feed before reviewing assignments during shift handoffs to ensure your team is working from the most current visit data.
</Tip>
